Casim Yazıcı

I'm an assistant professor at civil engineering department, Ağrı İbrahim Çeçen University. I obtained my PhD degree at Atatürk University supervised by Fatih Mehmet Ozkal.

I am interested in storage rack systems. My doctoral thesis topic was about combinations of storage rack systems and system analysis. CV available upon request.
